About JCSC IT Club

Born from a passion for technology and a belief in Bangladesh's digital future, JCSC IT Club is where tomorrow's innovators take their first steps.

Our Mission

JCSC IT Club was founded in 2025 with a clear mission: to nurture and empower the next generation of digital leaders at Jolshiri Cantonment School and College.

Through hands-on workshops, competitive hackathons, and a gamified learning ecosystem, we transform curious students into confident creators.

We're not just a club — we're a launchpad. Whether you dream of building AI systems, designing apps, or securing networks, your tech legacy starts here.

📅 Journey

Our Timeline

JANUARY 2025
Club Founded
JCSC IT Club officially established with a founding panel of 8 dedicated student leaders.
MARCH 2025
First Workshop Series
Launched our inaugural coding workshop series covering HTML/CSS, Python basics, and AI. Over 50 students enrolled.
JUNE 2025
Tech Legacy Website Launch
Launched our interactive Tech Legacy platform — a gamified ecosystem to track member progress and map Bangladesh's tech future.
AUGUST 2025
First Hackathon
Hosting JCSC's first-ever intra-school hackathon with 48 hours of coding, design, and hardware challenges.
FUTURE 2026+
National Stage
Competing at national IT olympiads and establishing JCSC as Bangladesh's premier school tech club.
